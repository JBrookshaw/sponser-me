Message To the Universe

We want to build a web application that will enable students who are passionate about learning to be able to use it as a tool for self-promotion, networking and portfolio/ experience building. The app will allow them to find people who are willing to fund their education or passion projects. The main purpose of this web application is to alleviate the amount of student debt that our country has accumulated. Students who are entering college can create an account on this app and will be able to create a bio which states their major, interests, hobbies, achievements, ongoing projects, completed projects, resume, portfolio and photos that describe what they are passionate about and what they want to learn more about.
